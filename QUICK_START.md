## 📁 WHERE TO PUT YOUR FILES

### **Location in File Explorer:**
```
D:\Portfolio\public\
├── images\
│   ├── profile.jpg    ← PUT YOUR PROFILE PHOTO HERE
│   └── about.jpg      ← PUT YOUR ABOUT PHOTO HERE (or use same as profile)
└── resume.pdf         ← PUT YOUR RESUME HERE
```

### **Step-by-Step:**

1. **Open File Explorer**
2. **Navigate to:** `D:\Portfolio\public\images\`
3. **Copy your profile photo** and rename it to: `profile.jpg`
4. **Copy your about photo** (can be same) and rename to: `about.jpg`
5. **Go back to** `D:\Portfolio\public\`
6. **Copy your resume PDF** and rename to: `resume.pdf`
7. **Refresh browser** - Done! ✅

---

## 📸 Image Specifications

### Profile Photo (`profile.jpg`):
- **Size:** 300x300 pixels (square)
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Style:** Professional headshot, good lighting
- **Background:** Plain or blurred

### About Photo (`about.jpg`):
- **Size:** 400x400 pixels (square)
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Tip:** Can use the same as profile.jpg

### Resume (`resume.pdf`):
- **Format:** PDF only
- **Size:** Under 2MB
- **Include:** Contact info, skills, projects, education
